I am a Staff Frontend Engineer with advanced expertise in React, TypeScript, CSS, and a wide range of frontend frameworks. My passion lies in bridging the gap between design and development—collaborating closely with designers to translate their visions into accessible and delightful user experiences. My commitment to accessibility ensures that everyone can engage with the experiences I build.

I enjoy using design tools like Figma for collaboration, ensuring that what gets deployed honors the designer’s vision without compromise.

As both a creative and analytical thinker, I consider the user’s perspective in every decision, whether it’s solving complex technical challenges or refining the smallest UI elements.

If you’re looking for a frontend engineer with a remarkable ability to work in lock-step with designers and product teams, <LinkButton href="https://www.linkedin.com/in/rachelcantor/">let’s connect</LinkButton>!
